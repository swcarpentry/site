Our mission is to teach researchers basic lab skills for scientific computing: the tools and techniques that will help them get more done in less time, and with less pain. Our volunteer instructors have run hundreds of events for thousands of scientists in the past two and a half years. All of our lesson materials are freely reusable under the Creative Commons - Attribution license.

Supporters

Software Carpentry is made possible by the generous support of these supporters, and by the hard work of all our volunteers. If you would like to support us, we offer several levels of engagement.

Foundation

Software Carpentry is a member organization of NumFOCUS, a 501(c)3 non-profit in the United States. Please see our by-laws and other documents for more information.
