We are very grateful to NumFOCUS for sheltering our work. Its aim to promote the use of accessible and reproducible computing in science and technology through educational programs, the promotion of high-level languages, reproducible scientific research, and open-code development, and an increase in collaborative research tools and documentation.
Data Carpentry is a sibling organization of Software Carpentry that teaches basic concepts, skills and tools for working more effectively with data. They develop curricula and run workshops that are domain specific, target fundamental data analysis and data management challenges, and require little or no prior programming experience.
