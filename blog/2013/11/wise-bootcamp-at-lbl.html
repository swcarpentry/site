We are very pleased to announce that our second bootcamp for women in science and engineering will be held at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory on April 14-15, 2014. This event is a sequel to our first WiSE bootcamp in Boston this past June, and we hope to once again fill three rooms with over 120 learners. If you are interested, please mark the dates in your calendar—registration will open in January.
