We are pleased to announce that we will be running workshops simultaneously at the data science centers at UC Berkeley, NYU, and the University of Washington on March 17-18, 2014. These workshops will introduce participants to the skills they should master before they tackle anything with "cloud" or "peta" in its name, including task automation, version control, modular programming, and structured data management. Novice and intermediate learners alike are welcome: we will stream attendees into different rooms using a short questionnaire to make sure everyone gets as much as possible out of their two days. For more information on topics and other details, please mail us or check out the registration pages for the three sites:

  • UC Berkeley
  • New York University
