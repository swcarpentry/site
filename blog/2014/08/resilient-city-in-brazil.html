"Resilient City" is the central theme of the 2nd International Meeting on Urban Environment (Enintau), which will be held between 6 and August 8 at the Pontifical Catholic University of São Paulo (PUC-SP). The event is organized by the Group of Urban Environment Research (CNPq / PUC-SP), coordinated by Professor Daniela Campos Liborio Di Sarno. The meeting is aimed at undergraduate and postgraduate students and professionals and researchers in the fields of Law, Geography, Economics, Geology, Sociology, Urban Planning, Engineering, and other disciplines related to the study and management of the Urban Environment. For more information, please see http://www.enintau.blogspot.com.br/.
